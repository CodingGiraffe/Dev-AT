About Me

Born in Dallas, I moved to Austin, Tx when I was just four years old. I grew up in the Lake Travis area and remained there until I graduated high school. Growing up in (a much smaller) Austin was incredible.

Next, I was off to college at Texas State University in San Marcos, Tx. Here I graduated in four years with a BBA and deans list honors. After college, I moved back to Austin and became a Realtor until the market crash of 2009. It was time for a new adventure.

In 2011, I was offered a job in Denver, CO at the headquaters of WildBlue Communications, a satellite company. After a couple years an old friend asked me to be a part of his company Evolved Ideas which builds websites and offers SEO and PPC ad campaigns. I truly loved living in Colorado. From skiing to fishing and everthing in betweeen, Colorado was a fantastic experience in my life.

Which brings me to today. I am currently enrolled at Austin Coding Academy to further my knowledge in the tech industry. I know if I use the same hard work and dedication I've used in everything else in my life, I will no doubt reach my goal of becoming a full time developer
